FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I contribute to Distributed Knowledge?

There are several ways you can contribute to distributed knowledge. If you would like to provide content to the site, please submit a pull request here. If you're not sure what kind of information to add, please visit our 'Future Content' page.

How can I trust the information on Distributed Knowledge?

Distributed Knowledge runs on the assumption of Linus's Law, "given enough eyeballs, all bugs are shallow." With a healthy community of contributers and moderators, blatant erros will be avoided and those that slip by will be caught by members of the community and corrected shortly after.
